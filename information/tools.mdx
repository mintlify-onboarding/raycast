title: "Tools"
---

Raycast provides several tools to smoothen your experience when building extensions:

* [CLI](/information/tools/cli) _\- A CLI to build, develop, and lint your extension_

* [ESLint](/information/tools/eslint) _\- An ESLint configuration helping you follow best practices as you build your extension_

* [VS Code (community tool)](/information/tools/vscode) _\- A VS Code extension to enhance your development experience_
